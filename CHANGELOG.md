### Fixed

- **Customer shell drew two app bars on the Bookings and More tabs** (2026-07-09) ŌĆö a folder reorg reintroduced full-page `Scaffold` + `AppTopBar` wrappers on `MyBookingsScreen` and `ProfileScreen`, which the shell then embedded as tab bodies **inside its own `Scaffold`** ŌĆö so those two tabs rendered a second app bar. It also left two files declaring `MyBookingsScreen` (`presentation/` and `presentation/pages/`), an ambiguous-import compile error; the stale `presentation/` copy was already broken (it imported a deleted widget) and was deleted. Split both screens along the pattern the booking feature already used (`LiveBookingScreen` wraps `BookingView`): the routed `MyBookingsScreen` (`/my-bookings`) and `ProfileScreen` (`/profile`) keep their `Scaffold` + `AppTopBar`, while the new scaffold-less `MyBookingsView` and `ProfileView` hold the content and are what the shell embeds. Standalone routes keep their app bar and back button; the shell keeps exactly one of each. Added a widget test that taps through the customer tabs asserting one `AppBar` + one `AppBottomNavBar` per tab. `flutter analyze` clean; all 127 tests pass.
